Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to your café?

Our café is located at the adress: 123 Coffee Lane, Coffee City. You can find us easily in the heart of Coffee City. We're just a few minutes walk from the Central Park

What are your opening hours?

We are open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and on weekends from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Feel free to stop by anytime!

Do I need to make a reservation?

We don't require reservations. However, if you're coming with a large group, feel free to give us a call at +123 456 7890 to ensure we have space available for you.

What types of coffee do you serve?

We offer a variety of freshly brewed coffee, including espresso, cappuccino, latte, mocha, and more. We also have a selection of non-coffee beverages like tea, hot chocolate, and smoothies.

Do you have options for dietary restrictions?

Yes, we have dairy-free and gluten-free options available. Ask our staff for recommendations when you visit!

Can I take my coffee to go?

Absolutely! You can enjoy your coffee in-house or take it to go. Just let us know how you'd like it when you order.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer gift cards in various denominations. Feel free to ask at the counter for more details!

Do you have Wi-Fi at the café?

Yes, we offer free Wi-Fi to our customers. Simply ask for the Wi-Fi password when you visit!

Can I bring my pet?

We love pets! While we have a pet-friendly outdoor seating area, we kindly ask that pets stay outside the indoor seating area for everyone's comfort.

Do you offer catering or special events?

Yes, we provide catering for small events and parties. Please reach out to us at +123 456 7890 or stop by to discuss your event needs.
